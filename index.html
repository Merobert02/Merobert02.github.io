Where did I learn to draw?

I began drawing at a pretty young age. I went to BJU and graduated with my BFA and began my own business from there. I have learned more about the Fine Art society than I ever expected to, and while I am in it, I do belive I am making a concious effort to be seperate. I never want my artwork to feel like it excludes folkd from loving art or trying it out for themselves. My focus is on the community I am building around my artwork.

What is my work about?

My work centers around the beauty in ordinary objects. My favorite consists of glass and sky. I love to take photos of glasses when I'm out at a coffee shop or with friends for dinner. There is something about the beauty of light reflecting in glass that seems so complex yet so delicate at the same time. To capture that in an atmosphere of effortless sky creates and image of beauty in nothing more than light and color.
